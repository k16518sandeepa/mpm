About Us

Motion Picture Mafia Studio (MPM) is Sri Lanka's premier entertainment community — your go-to source for Movies, TV series, Anime, gaming and Tech. Our mission is simple: bring fans the best reviews, news, and updates in the entertainment world, all in Sinhala.

Our Mission

We believe great entertainment content should be accessible to everyone — especially Sri Lankan fans who want to follow global film, TV, and gaming culture in their own language. MPM covers it all, from Hollywood blockbusters to indie anime gems, in Sinhala.

What We Offer

  • 🎬 Movie reviews, trailers and box office updates
  • 📺 TV Series episode guides and season breakdowns
  • ⭐ Anime news, season schedules and character deep-dives
  • 🎮 Gaming news, release schedules and game downloads
  • 🔬 Cutting-edge science news, tech tutorials and hardware reviews
  • 📥 Sinhala subtitle downloads and a subtitle translator tool
  • 🆓 Free game deal alerts from Epic, Steam and more

Why Choose MPM?

  • ⚡ Fast and reliable updates — we post before anyone else
  • 🎯 Honest, community driven reviews with no paid promotions
  • 🇱🇰 Written in Sinhala for Sri Lankan fans, by Sri Lankan fans
